Documentation of my IxD Process

This page outlines the thinking and design principles I applied to create my website.

Style Guides

Goal

My goal is to have a user-friendly, accessible, and clear website that reflects who I am as a designer.

Visual Design Elements

I aim to create strong typographic hierarchy using the ‘Grown’ font for headings to convey a soft, feminine tone, and ‘Montserrat Classic’ for body text for clarity. I also maintain a limited color palette with brown (to embrace identity), orange and yellow (for warmth), and cool accents (for contrast).

Implementation Notes

  • I created a hierarchy where screen titles appear first, center-aligned for balance.
  • Bright yellow buttons are used to draw attention to interaction points.
  • Cards on the blog page highlight on hover for interactivity.
  • Scrolling allows easy navigation through longer content.
